NAD+ Injections

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a vital coenzyme found in every cell of the body. It powers cellular energy production, drives DNA repair, and activates longevity enzymes. Levels decline naturally with age — NAD+ therapy replenishes them directly via injection for maximum bioavailability, bypassing the digestive system entirely for more predictable, full-strength results.

  • Sharper mental focus & relief from brain fog
  • Restored energy via mitochondrial support (ATP production)
  • Supports healthy aging and DNA repair mechanisms
  • Activates SIRT1 — the body's longevity enzyme
  • Higher bioavailability vs oral supplements — injections bypass digestion
  • Full-strength dosage: 500mg–1000mg per vial (200mg/mL)

Peptide Therapy · Injectable

Sermorelin Injections

Sermorelin is a synthetic peptide that mimics growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH), stimulating the pituitary gland to naturally produce and release growth hormone. As HGH levels decline with age, Sermorelin offers a science-based approach to supporting lean body composition, recovery, and metabolic wellness — without anabolic steroids.

  • Supports lean muscle growth and weight management alongside exercise
  • Restores natural energy levels and reduces fatigue throughout the day
  • Promotes restful sleep and faster post-workout recovery
  • May support skin health, elasticity, and bone density over time
  • Stimulates natural HGH pathways — not synthetic hormone replacement
  • 5mL (10mg) vial, home kit included, free expedited shipping
