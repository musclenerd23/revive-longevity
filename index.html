NAD+ Injections

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a vital coenzyme found in every cell of the body. It powers cellular energy production, drives DNA repair, and activates longevity enzymes. Levels decline naturally with age — NAD+ therapy replenishes them directly via injection for maximum bioavailability. Unlike oral supplements, injections bypass digestion entirely for more predictable, full-strength results.

  • Sharper mental focus & relief from brain fog
  • Restored energy via mitochondrial support (ATP production)
  • Supports healthy aging and DNA repair mechanisms
  • Activates SIRT1 — the body's longevity enzyme
  • Higher bioavailability vs oral supplements
  • Full-strength dose: 500mg–1000mg per vial (200mg/mL)
