FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before starting your protocol.

We currently offer BPC-157, Sermorelin, NAD+, Thymosin Alpha-1, PT-141, and Ipamorelin (including CJC-1295 stacks). All peptides are prescribed after a full clinical review by a licensed provider and compounded in FDA-registered US pharmacies.

Peptides are generally well-tolerated when dosed correctly. Most are naturally occurring compounds identical or similar to those your body already produces. Common minor side effects include temporary injection site reactions or mild flushing. Your provider reviews your health history before prescribing and monitors your response throughout your protocol.

We currently serve all 50 US states. Our licensed providers are authorized to prescribe across the country, and we ship to every US state with free expedited cold-chain delivery on all protocols.

We don't bill insurance — our care model is designed to operate outside the traditional healthcare system for speed, access, and transparency. However, most of our protocols are FSA and HSA eligible, meaning you can use pre-tax health dollars to cover your treatment costs.

Timeline varies by protocol and individual. NAD+ often produces energy and clarity improvements within the first 1–2 sessions. BPC-157 shows tissue repair effects within 2–4 weeks. Sermorelin and Ipamorelin typically show body composition changes within 4–8 weeks of consistent use. Your provider will set realistic expectations based on your specific protocol.

Every plan includes your provider consultation, full protocol design, prescription medications compounded to your dose, free cold-chain shipping, injection supplies, and ongoing provider check-ins. There are no hidden fees — the price you see includes everything required to run your protocol.
